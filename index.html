Welcome to My Little Steps

At My Little Steps Playschool, we believe that every child is unique and deserves a personalized approach to learning. Our play-based curriculum is designed to nurture curiosity, creativity, and confidence in children aged 2-5 years.

Established in 2010, our school provides a safe, stimulating environment where children can explore, discover, and grow at their own pace. Our experienced educators are passionate about early childhood development and committed to helping each child reach their full potential.
