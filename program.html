Our Learning Programs

Nurturing young minds through play-based learning, creative exploration, and personalized care.

Specialized Programs for Each Age Group

At My Little Steps Playschool, we believe every child is unique and deserves an education tailored to their developmental stage. Our age-appropriate programs are designed to foster cognitive, social, emotional, and physical growth in a safe, nurturing environment.

Toddler Program

Ages 2-3 Years

Our Toddler Program is specially designed for our youngest learners, ages 2-3 years. During these formative years, children experience rapid development in language, motor skills, and social abilities. Our nurturing environment provides the perfect balance of structure and freedom for toddlers to explore, learn, and grow.

Program Features:

  • Low child-to-teacher ratio (maximum 1:4) ensuring personalized attention
  • Developmentally appropriate activities focusing on sensory exploration
  • Introduction to basic concepts through play-based learning
  • Daily language development exercises and storytelling sessions
  • Fine and gross motor skill development through structured play
  • Music, movement, and creative expression activities
  • Potty training support in partnership with parents
  • Socialization opportunities in small group settings

Our toddler environments are thoughtfully designed with age-appropriate materials that encourage curiosity and independence. Each day includes a balance of teacher-led activities, free play, outdoor exploration, and rest time.

Half-day: 8:30am-12:30pm
Full-day: 8:30am-3:30pm
Extended care available

Preschool Program

Ages 3-5 Years

Our Preschool Program builds upon the foundational skills developed in our toddler program and prepares children ages 3-5 for kindergarten success. We implement a comprehensive curriculum that balances academic readiness with social-emotional development, creative expression, and physical activity.

Program Features:

  • Literacy-rich environment with reading and writing readiness activities
  • Introduction to mathematical concepts through hands-on activities
  • Science exploration and discovery through experiments and nature studies
  • Project-based learning opportunities that follow children's interests
  • Social studies and cultural awareness activities
  • Development of self-regulation and problem-solving skills
  • Technology introduction with supervised, educational screen time
  • Art, music, and dramatic play opportunities for creative expression
  • Physical education activities to develop coordination and strength

Our preschool curriculum is designed to foster independence, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning. Children participate in both whole-group and small-group activities, with plenty of opportunities for individual choice and exploration.

Half-day: 8:30am-12:30pm
Full-day: 8:30am-3:30pm
Extended care available

Benefits for Parents & Children

When you enroll your child at My Little Steps Playschool, both you and your child will experience numerous advantages that support your family's well-being and your child's development.

  • Early Social Development

    Children learn essential social skills like sharing, taking turns, and cooperating with peers. These early friendships and social interactions lay the groundwork for healthy relationships throughout life.

  • Academic Readiness

    Our age-appropriate curriculum introduces foundational concepts in literacy, mathematics, and science, giving your child a head start for kindergarten and beyond.

  • Consistent Routine & Structure

    Children thrive with consistency. Our predictable daily schedule helps children feel secure while developing time-management skills and preparing them for the structure of elementary school.

  • Professional Support & Guidance

    Our experienced educators provide expert guidance on child development, behavior management, and educational best practices, offering parents valuable insights and support.

  • Work-Life Balance

    With flexible scheduling options and extended care hours, parents can maintain professional commitments while knowing their children are receiving quality care and education.
